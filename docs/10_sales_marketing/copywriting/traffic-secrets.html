4. Fill Your Funnel (The 3-Step Process)

How to systematically drive traffic to any funnel

Core Concept: Filling your funnel isn't random. Follow this 3-step process for EVERY funnel you build.

  • Step 1: WHO is your dream customer? (Demographics + psychographics - be SPECIFIC)
  • Step 2: WHERE are they congregating? (Your Dream 100 - podcasts, YouTube, Facebook groups, etc.)
  • Step 3: What BAIT will attract them? (Your hook - what will make them click/opt-in/watch?)

Russell's ClickFunnels Example:

  • WHO: Entrepreneurs making $100K-$1M/year who want to scale online
  • WHERE: Marketing podcasts, YouTube (Neil Patel, Gary Vee), Facebook groups for entrepreneurs
  • BAIT: Free book "DotCom Secrets" (+ $7.95 shipping)

5. Infiltrating the Dream 100 (Tactical Playbook)

Specific tactics to get in front of your Dream 100's audience

Work Your Way In (Organic):

  • Podcast Guesting: Pitch yourself as a guest (provide value to THEIR audience)
  • Guest Blogging: Write articles for Dream 100 websites
  • Joint Webinars: Co-host webinars with influencers (split the leads)
  • Affiliate Partnerships: Offer generous commissions for promoting your offer
  • Social Media Engagement: Comment, share, tag (build relationship first)

Buy Your Way In (Paid):

  • Podcast Sponsorships: Pay to have your ad read on their podcast
  • Facebook Ads: Target fans of Dream 100 (Custom Audiences)
  • YouTube Ads: Run ads on Dream 100 videos
  • Banner Ads: Buy ad space on their websites
  • Sponsored Emails: Pay to send email to their list (solo ads)

Russell's Rule: "If I can't work my way in within 30 days, I buy my way in with ads."

6. Platform-Specific Traffic Strategies

How to adapt Hook/Story/Offer for each major platform

Russell documents detailed strategies for every major platform. Here's the summary:

Search Traffic (Google, YouTube, Blogs):

  • Intent: People searching for solutions (problem-aware)
  • Strategy: SEO content, YouTube videos answering questions
  • Hook: Answer the search query directly
  • Best For: Evergreen traffic (long-term asset)

Social Traffic (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok):

  • Intent: Entertainment/browsing (not actively searching)
  • Strategy: Interrupt with pattern-breaking content
  • Hook: Scroll-stopping (visuals, curiosity, bold claims)
  • Best For: Brand awareness, reaching cold audiences

Email Traffic (Your List):

  • Intent: Already know/trust you (warm/hot traffic)
  • Strategy: Soap Opera Sequences (DotCom Secrets), Epiphany Bridge stories
  • Hook: Subject line (curiosity + benefit)
  • Best For: Converting warm traffic to customers
