5. The Summit Funnel (Virtual Event Lead Gen)

Core Concept: Host a multi-day virtual event where you interview 20-30 experts. Attendees register (free) to watch. You generate MASSIVE lead lists fast + build authority by association with experts.

The Summit Strategy:

  • Step 5: Run summit (release interviews daily over 5 days)
