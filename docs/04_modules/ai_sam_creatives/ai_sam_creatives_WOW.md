### How It Works (The Simple Version)

1. **Open the Creatives Canvas** - Start with a blank workspace or existing project
2. **Add multimedia nodes** - Drop in text, images, videos, audio clips
3. **Chat with SAM** - Ask for ideas, refinements, or completely new directions
4. **Organize visually** - Drag nodes, connect ideas, build your story
5. **Export and share** - Take your creation wherever it needs to go

### Part of the SAM AI Ecosystem

SAM AI Creatives is a Platform Skin built on the SAM Canvas framework:

| Module | What It Adds | How It Connects |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| **ai_brain** | Data storage and canvas models | Stores all your creative content |
| **ai_sam** | Canvas framework + AI chat | Powers the rendering and AI |
| **ai_sam_creatives** | **Multimedia creative experience** | **You are here** |
| **ai_sam_socializer** | Social media publishing | Share your creations |

**Together, they make content creation intuitive and intelligent.**

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where is my content stored?**
A: All content is stored in the ai_brain module. Creatives is just the UI layer - your data is always safe.

**Q: Can I use this without AI chat?**
A: Yes! The canvas works perfectly for visual organization without using the AI features.

**Q: What media formats are supported?**
A: Images (PNG, JPG, GIF), video embeds, audio files, and rich text.
