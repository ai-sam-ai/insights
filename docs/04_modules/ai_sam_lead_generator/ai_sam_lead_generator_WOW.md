### How It Works (The Simple Version)

1. **Create a campaign** - Choose your targets (industries, countries, Odoo versions)
2. **Configure sources** - Enable Google search, forums, job boards, GitHub
3. **Run the campaign** - ScraperAPI handles the heavy lifting
4. **Review results** - Scored leads with Odoo detection details
5. **Import to CRM** - Hot leads go directly to your pipeline

**No manual searching. No copy-pasting. Just qualified leads.**
