### How It Works (The Simple Version)

1. **Create a campaign** - Set your goals, audience, and tone
2. **Chat with SAM** - Describe what you want, upload files, or paste content
3. **Refine iteratively** - Keep chatting until the email is perfect
4. **Create mailing** - One click to generate a real Odoo email campaign
5. **Send and track** - Use Odoo's native sending and analytics

**No templates to maintain. No coding required. Just conversation.**

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which AI models are supported?**
A: The module is LLM-agnostic. Configure your preferred provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) in ai_sam settings.

**Q: Can I edit the AI-generated content?**
A: Absolutely. Every template is fully editable before creating a mailing.

**Q: Does it track costs?**
A: Yes. Every generation request logs tokens used and estimated cost.
