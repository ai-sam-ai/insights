### How It Works (The Simple Version)

1. **Pick a Template** - Choose from 6 funnel types based on your goal (grow list, sell product, book webinars)

2. **Drag and Customize** - Add, remove, and reorder 38 funnel snippets in the website builder

3. **Connect Your CRM** - Select which team gets the leads and which mailing list gets subscribers

4. **Let SAM Write** - Describe your offer and SAM generates conversion-focused copy

**That's it.** No coding. No third-party subscriptions. No export/import headaches.
