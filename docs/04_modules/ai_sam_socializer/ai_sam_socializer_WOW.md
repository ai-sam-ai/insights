### How It Works (The Simple Version)

1. **Create Your Post** - Write your blog content with the rich text editor, add a featured image, and organize your story flow on the story board

2. **Customize Variations** - Jump to the FB Variation tab and tailor your content for each platform (Facebook allows long-form, Twitter needs 280 characters, YouTube wants a description)

3. **Preview & Publish** - Use the Visualizer to check how it looks, then hit the platform buttons to push your content live

**That's it.** No jumping between apps. No copy-paste errors. No forgotten platforms.
